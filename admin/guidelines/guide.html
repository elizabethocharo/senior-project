Guidelines for Getting the URL to Embed a Location in Google Maps:

  1. Open Google Maps in your web browser.
  2. Search for the location you want to embed.
  3. Click on the location marker to open the details.
  4. Click the "Share" button or select "Embed a map" option.
  5. Copy the URL provided in the embed code.
  6. Paste the URL in the form
